Bit of Farming is a foray into making games in 64x64 pixels made for Low Rez Jam 2016.
Tested in Chrome, should work in Firefox, probably wont work in Explorer, Edge, or Safari.
WASD to move, E to select, Q for inventory or to exit a menu. M to pause / unpause music.
Audio by Brett Coop
Art by Peter Foster
Programming by Ian Hern
